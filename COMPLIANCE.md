\# Regulatory Compliance Framework

\## UAE FinPay — Project 1



\## CBUAE SAR/STR Framework (June 2026)



\### Legal Basis

\- Federal Decree-Law No. (10) of 2025 — Primary AML/CFT/CPF legislation

\- Cabinet Resolution No. (134) of 2025 — Executive Regulations

\- CBUAE AML/CFT/CPF Guidance Package — April 16, 2026



\### Critical Clarification — No Monetary Threshold

SAR/STR filing is triggered by REASONABLE SUSPICION not by

transaction amount. A transaction of AED 1 is as reportable

as AED 1,000,000 if suspicion exists.



Internal escalation flags (AED 40k and 100k) in this project

are operational monitoring triggers only. They are NOT

SAR/STR thresholds per CBUAE 2026 guidance.



\### Behavior-Based Detection Applied in This Project

\- Structuring patterns: multiple sub-threshold transactions

&#x20; designed to avoid detection

\- Velocity anomalies: sudden spike vs 90-day customer baseline

\- Dormant account reactivation with unusual high-value activity

\- KYC failures and pending verification flags

\### Key 2026 Regulatory Updates

\- TBML: Trade-Based Money Laundering — first standalone guidance

\- PF: Proliferation Financing — mandatory standalone risk assessment

\- Dynamic CDD: continuous monitoring not one-time onboarding

\- RegTech Focus: zero tolerance for poor data quality in goAML

\- FATF 5th Round: UAE preparing for 2026 Mutual Evaluation
